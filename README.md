# Daily Planner

A lightweight personal task manager built as a Progressive Web App (PWA). It helps you stay on top of your to-do list without feeling overwhelmed — giving you a focused set of tasks each day rather than an endless backlog.

## Features

- **Daily focus list** — each day you get a prioritized plan of 3–5 tasks to tackle, chosen automatically based on priority and how long they've been sitting on your list
- **One-time tasks** — add individual tasks with high, medium, or low priority
- **Recurring tasks** — set tasks that repeat daily, on specific days of the week, or on a day each month (e.g. "do laundry every Wednesday")
- **Missed task handling** — if you miss a recurring task, the app will ask if you want to mark it done, skip it, or reschedule
- **Push notifications** — get a morning briefing at 7:30am with your daily plan and a gentle evening reminder at 5:00pm for anything still open
- **Works offline** — once installed, the app works without an internet connection
- **Installable** — add it to your phone's home screen for a native app feel

## How to use it

### Adding tasks

Go to the **Add** tab. You can add a one-time task with a title and priority, or set up a recurring task with a schedule. Tap **Add task** or **Add recurring task** to save it.

### Your daily plan

The **Today** tab shows your focus list for the day — a short, prioritized selection of what to tackle. Recurring tasks that are due today appear automatically at the top marked with 🔁.

### Checking things off

Tap the circle to the left of any task to mark it complete. On recurring tasks this resets each day so it shows up again next time it's due.

### Notifications

Go to the **Settings** tab and tap **Enable** under notifications. You can adjust the morning and evening notification times there too. Tap **Send test** to make sure everything is working.

### Installing on your phone

Open the app URL in Chrome on Android or Safari on iOS, then use the browser's "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app" option. Once installed, open it from your home screen for the best experience.
